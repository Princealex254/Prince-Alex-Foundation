Empowering Communities, Building Futures

The Prince Alex Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering positive change in Vihiga County, Kenya. Founded by Alex Senerwa, we uplift communities through education, health, and youth empowerment initiatives.

Learn More Get Involved

Welcome to Our Foundation

We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to thrive. Our programs address the most pressing needs of our community — providing access to quality education, promoting public health, and empowering the next generation of leaders. Join us in making a lasting impact.

Our Core Initiatives

Education

Providing scholarships and school supplies to deserving students to ensure they have the tools to succeed.

Health

Organizing health camps and awareness campaigns to promote wellness and prevent disease in our communities.

Youth Empowerment

Mentoring young leaders and offering skills training to equip them for a brighter future.
