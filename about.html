Our Story

The Journey of Prince Alex Foundation

Founded by visionary leader **Alex Senerwa**, also the founder of Prince Alex Digital, the Prince Alex Foundation was born from a deep-seated desire to give back to the community that shaped him. Alex witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by many in Vihiga County, Kenya, and was inspired to create a vehicle for sustainable change. Our journey began with small-scale projects, and through dedication and community support, we have grown into an organization with a broad reach and a lasting impact.

We believe that true empowerment comes from within. Our work is not about providing temporary fixes, but about building long-term capacity and fostering self-reliance within the communities we serve.

Our Mission

To uplift and empower communities in Vihiga County by providing access to essential resources, fostering education, and promoting sustainable development.

Our Vision

A future where every individual in Vihiga County has the opportunity to lead a healthy, educated, and dignified life, contributing to a prosperous society.
